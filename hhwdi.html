Ho Ho Who Did It?

Role(s): Audio Designer
Software Used: Garageband
Release Date: November 7, 2021
Website
Soundtrack



Responsibilities:

  • Composed the entire soundtrack, consisting of 7 tracks total
  • Suggested that puzzles did not need to be square, thereby saving the artists and programmers time that would’ve been spent making more puzzle pieces
  • Created sound effects for pushing and pulling puzzle pieces
  • Created sound effects for the player walk cycle

Entry to the weekend-long MEGA Newbies and Vets Game Jam 2022 with the given theme constraint: “restore.” Awarded “Best Audio.” Santa Claus has been murdered and the player must restore 3 pieces of evidence presented in the form of jigsaw-like puzzles to uncover Santa’s murderer.
