Your cargo ship has run out of power and is headed straight toward [an unspecified] Bridge! Use your mouse to move the cargo on board to control the ship's balance and steer it in the correct direction. Beware of birds flying around trying to snatch your cargo! Smack birds with cargo to ward them off. Be careful! If the ship is too unbalanced, it may capsize.
