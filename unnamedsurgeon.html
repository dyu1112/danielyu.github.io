Responsibilities:

  • Implemented most gameplay features within the last 3 hours
  • Implemented functionality for picking up and placing down interactable items
  • Recorded and implemented voiceover for all sound effects
  • Implemented a win/lose condition and lives system
  • Implemented a gradually dimming light that can be reactivated through interaction

Entry to the 2024 Global Game Jam at USC Games, with the theme requirement: "Make Me Laugh." In this game, the player needs to find surgical instruments that make funny sounds to keep the light on. If the light goes off, the player loses a life. The player loses when they run out of lives or the light remains off for too long.
