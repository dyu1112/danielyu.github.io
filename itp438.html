ITP 438 - Advanced Gameplay Programming

Role(s): Programmer
Software Used: Unreal Engine 5
Aug. 2023 - Dec. 2023



Responsibilities:

  • Implemented a 3rd-person interaction system that chooses the closest interactable object that is most directly in front of the player and within range
  • Implemented dash, death, crouch, and prone animations and states
  • Tracked and updated the player's "threat level", such that crouching, being prone, and standing in stealth box areas decreased threat level, and moving increases threat level
  • Implemented enemy AI through a behavior tree with two states, Chase and Patrol, based on the player's threat level and whether the player is within the enemy's line of sight
  • Implemented listen server networking with Steam and dedicated server networking
  • Implemented fair team-making and spawning
  • Replicated each team's overall score across all clients and kept track of each client's individual score
  • Implemented respawns in different locations if an opponent is too close to the player's team's spawn point to prevent spawn camping
  • Implemented a match chat viewable by everybody and a team chat viewable by the player's team
  • Utilized Online Subsystem Steam to allow players to search and join their Steam friend's session
  • Added the ability to spray tags in the world and autosave the tags's locations to be loaded in future matches
  • Added the ability to launch a grenade that kills all players within a certain range after 3 seconds
