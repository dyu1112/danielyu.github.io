DuoQ

Role(s): Audio Lead
Technologies Used: C++, Unreal Engine 5, MetaSounds
Apr. 2024 - Present



Responsibilities:

  • Sonic Identity Development: Conceptualized and maintained the game’s sonic identity through an audio bible, asset sheets, and audio system diagrams, creating a clear, consistent vision for a 4-member audio team
  • Sound Design: Executed the sonic identity and developed 10+ sound assets for an in-game character using Ableton Live and Reaper
  • Adaptive Music: Designed and deployed a hybrid vertical and horizontal music system that responds to player performance and progression utilizing Unreal MetaSounds and C++ scripting
  • Dynamic Foley: Deployed dynamic physics-based foley and sound effects based on terrain type, resulting in a 30% increase in auditory realism and immersion
  • Voiceover Dialogue: Spearheaded recording and integration of 30+ voiceover dialogue lines in Unreal Engine 5, resulting in heightened realism and more human-like player-NPC interactions

DuoQ is an upcoming puzzle dating sim with FPS elements where the player works together with an LLM-powered partner, Starlight, to fight off hordes of enemies through voice commands to grow your relationship with Starlight.
